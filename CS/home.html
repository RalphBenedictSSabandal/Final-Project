RECYCOM

We're a non-profit organization seeking to promote recycling and reduce global waste. We provide a trash collecting service that recycles your trash with no extra fees at the same cost as average waste collecting services. The major difference? You receive refunds when you contribute enough to the service.

According to UNEP's Global Waste Management Outlook 2024 "Municipal solid waste generation is predicted to grow from 2.1 billion tonnes in 2023 to 3.8 billion tonnes by 2050. In 2020, the global direct cost of waste management was an estimated USD 252 billion."

We aim to work towards a world with less waste production. Waste can be reduced by simply recycling or reusing products. Our service works towards recycling waste from all around the world. So far we've extended our reach through all of Asia, Europe, and some of Africa. With your help we could extend our reach throughout the entire world, promoting recycling and lessening waste production along the way.

This is why you can help not only the world but also yourself and your family by subscribing to our service. You can even save money because of our refund feature. Simply contribute enough trash and our service is completely free. We hope you contribute to this cause that millions around the world are working towards, and you can join too.

WHY

RecyCom aims to work towards lessening waste production by promoting recycling to countries across the world.

WHERE

RecyCom is available across all of Asia, Europe and some countries in Africa namely Libya, Algeria, Tunisia, Egypt, South Africa, Morocco, and Botswana. We are currently working towards setting branches in the Americas, and that could be achieved with your help.

HOW

Simply subscribe to our service, provide your address, and we will collect your trash every 8am in your respective time zone. Contribute enough trash to our service, and you will receive refunds for your subscription, but it will not be cancelled unless you cancel it.
