HISTORY

Our project began with a small group of 5. We'd each go to local houses around the neighborhood and try to convince people to subscribe to our cheap service. We went through problems raising funds in the beginning, however many kind people gave life to the project through very generous donations. It began with the idea to promote and implement recycling all around the world, and we're still going. From 5 people, RecyCom has grown to around 200,000 personnel. We have been, and always will be a non-profit organization that aims to promote recycling to everyone in the world, and now, you can join us too.

VISION

To create a world where recycling is the norm, waste is limited, and the environment clean.

MISSION

To promote, encourage, and implement recycling throughout the world through a no-cost service.

ACHIEVEMENTS

Established locations through all of Asia and Europe, and some of Africa.

Recycled over 1.5 billion tons of trash, at a rate of around 21 million tons recycled per week currently.

Grown from a 5 man operation, to an organization comprised of over 200,000 people
